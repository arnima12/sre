BUP Robotics Club

BUPRC (BUP Robotics Club) was founded by a group of enthusiastic undergraduate students of department of Information and Communication Technology of BUP on September 13, 2017. BUP Robotics Club is one of the elite clubs of BUP organized with a view to developing latest talent of young minds regarding innovative research in the field of science and technology.
